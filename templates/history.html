THE HISTORY OF HEART DISEASE PREDICTION

From ancient observations to AI-powered digital health, the journey to save lives from heart disease is a story of innovation, science, and hope.

Ancient Times

Early physicians recognized the importance of the pulse and heart health, but had little understanding of heart disease causes or prevention.

19th Century

The invention of the stethoscope and advances in anatomy led to the first real diagnoses of heart conditions.

20th Century

Electrocardiograms (ECG), cholesterol testing, and large-scale studies (like Framingham) revealed risk factors and prevention strategies.

21st Century: Digital Health

AI, machine learning, and apps like VOXHEART now help predict heart disease risk instantly, empowering people to take control of their health.

18M+

People die from heart disease every year worldwide (WHO).

80%

Of premature heart attacks and strokes are preventable with lifestyle changes.

1 in 4

Deaths in the US are due to heart disease.
