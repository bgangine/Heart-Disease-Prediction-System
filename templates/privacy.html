Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data at VOXHEART.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the health data you enter for the purpose of providing heart health analysis. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to generate predictions and advice. No personal identifiers are stored unless you explicitly provide them.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage to remember your preferences (like dark mode and your name) for a better experience. No sensitive health data is stored in your browser.

5. Your Choices

You can clear your browser data at any time to remove your preferences. For questions, contact us at nrajulap@csu.edu.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated. Please check this page for changes.
